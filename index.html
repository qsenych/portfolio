About Me

I'm a fourth-year Computer Engineering student at UBC with a strong foundation in both firmware and electrical design, built through projects, student leadership, and co-op experience.

I've worked as an Electrical Designer at Eddyfi Robotics and an Electrical Test Engineering Co-op at Kardium Inc., gaining hands-on experience with confined space robotics and medical devices.

As the electrical and software lead of UBC ThunderBikes, I’ve led development of custom electrical and embedded systems for a fully custom electric motorcycle.
